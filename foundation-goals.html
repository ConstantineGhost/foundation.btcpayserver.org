Foundation Goals

The reality of financial sovereignty for every individual is closer than ever thanks to freely-available, open-source BTCPay Server.
The efforts of the Foundation and the support it receives are directed toward the following goals which seek to advance the ongoing development of the stack while meeting operational requirements.

Software Development

  • Development of new BTCPay Server features
  • Development and maintenance of e-commerce plugins
  • Production of training/tutorial material (Video, documentation...)
  • Promotion of BTCPay Server (events, hackathon, goodies)
  • Increase of the reach of BTCPay Server (helping it to run in more diverse environments)

Foundation Self-Sufficiency

  • Legal fees
  • Accountancy fees
  • Server maintenance costs

To learn more about the BTCPay Server Foundation visit the about us section.
